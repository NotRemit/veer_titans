About Us

We are the official Chess Club of VSSUT, Burla, where strategy meets passion and every move tells a story. Over the years, we've proudly brought home numerous victories and have made our mark in various inter-college and state-level tournaments.

Our club is powered by a team of dedicated minds, some of whom hold impressive FIDE ratings and have represented the university at prestigious platforms. Whether it's rapid, blitz, or classical — we don't just play, we compete with purpose.

We believe that chess is more than just a game — it's a battle of intellect, patience, and vision. Here, every pawn has potential, every player has promise, and every match is a step toward greatness.

“In life, as in chess, forethought wins.”

Whether you're a grandmaster in the making or just learning how to castle — there's a place for you on our board.

Join us, and make your next move the best one yet. ♟️
